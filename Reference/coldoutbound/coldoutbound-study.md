## 1. Brand Identity

### Name & Domain
- **Legal/Brand Name:** Growth Engine X (stylized "GROWTH ENGINE X" in nav)

- **Page Title:** "Growth Engine X - B2B Lead Generation"

## 3. Typography

### Font Families
| Font | Usage |
|------|-------|
| **Instrument Sans** | Primary display font — all headings, hero, section titles |
| **Inter** | Body copy, nav items, card text, fine print |

### Type Scale
| Element | Size | Weight | Color | Notes |
|---------|------|--------|-------|-------|
| H1 (hero) | 72px | 700 | White | Line-height 72px — tight, compressed |
| H2 (section) | 48px | 700 | White | e.g. "See Results Before You Commit" |
| H2 (client logo labels) | 18px | 600 | White | Smaller h2s used for card titles |
| H3 | Varies | 600 | White | Step labels, testimonial names |
| H4 | Smaller | 500 | White | Sub-methodology labels |
| Body / CTA | 16–18px | 400 | White / gray | Inter for all body text |
| Nav links | 14px | 400–500 | White | Inter, compact |
| CTA nav button | 14px | 500 | White on red | Inter, `10px 20px` padding |

### Typography Personality
- Zero-line-height gap on H1 — aggressive, impactful
- Instrument Sans gives a modern, geometric authority feel
- No serif anywhere — fully sans-serif stack
- Mixed size h2s is intentional: small h2s for logos = label-scale, big h2s for section = declaration-scale

**CTA Design Pattern:**
- Primary CTAs use an arrow `→` — implies forward motion

- Secondary CTAs use softer language ("Let's talk") for warmer leads

## 7. Messaging & Positioning

### Core Claim
> "We Send 8 Million+ Emails Per Month. We Know What Converts."

Volume = credibility. Not "we're the best agency" — "we send more than almost anyone and we've learned from it."

### Key Positioning Pillars
1. **Volume authority** — 8M+ emails/month, top 3 on Smartlead platform, Clay's largest user
2. **Verified by vendors** — Platform partners confirm the numbers (not just client claims)
3. **Risk-free entry** — Free test campaign removes the "what if it doesn't work" objection
4. **Specificity as proof** — "552 positive responses in 4 months" not "great results"
5. **Founder-led reputation** — "Eric" is the brand, not a faceless agency

### Value Proposition Hierarchy
1. We know what works (8M+ emails = real data)
2. We'll prove it before you pay (free test)
3. Our clients get specific measurable results (metrics grid)
4. Even our vendors vouch for us (platform partner section)
5. Here's the raw unfiltered proof (screenshot grid)

### Tone of Voice
- **Direct and confident** — no hedging, no "we believe" or "we think"
- **Data-first** — every claim has a number attached
- **Self-aware** — the Hormozi disclaimer ("not worked with, educational only") shows they're careful about implied associations
- **Anti-pretense** — the raw screenshots section explicitly names polished case studies as something to be skeptical of
- **Founder-peer** — reads like a respected practitioner, not a corporate agency
